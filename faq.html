FAQ

Specifications, flight regulations, integration, and operations.

What is the flight time of the RK-SPRAY drone?

Typical flight time is 18–22 minutes per battery at full payload. Hot-swappable batteries enable continuous operation with two or more packs.

How long does charging take?

Fast charge to 80% in approximately 35 minutes using the Rockio field charger. Full charge in under 60 minutes.

Is Rockio FAA-compliant in the US?

Rockio systems are designed for compliance with FAA Part 107 and applicable state agricultural regulations. Operators must hold a valid remote pilot certificate and any required state applicator licenses.

Can robots operate without internet connectivity?

Yes. Mission planning, execution, and logging work offline. Data syncs automatically when connectivity is restored.

Which farm management systems do you integrate with?

REST API and standard export formats. Direct integrations available for John Deere Operations Center and custom ERP connections on request.

How is drone imagery data handled?

Field data is processed under contract with the operator. We apply data minimization, encryption, and access controls. See our Privacy policy for details.

Is Rockio available for purchase today?

We are in active development and early deployment with select partners. Contact us to join the early access program.
