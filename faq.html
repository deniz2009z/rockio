RK-SPRAY intelligence bar — mounting, performance, safety, and early access.

What is the RK-SPRAY intelligence bar?

A retrofit spray module that mounts to a tractor toolbar or 3-point hitch. It adds global-shutter stereo cameras, onboard edge compute, and individually controlled PWM solenoid nozzles — so you spray targets, not the full row width.

How does it mount to my equipment?

The bar is designed as a standard-width toolbar implement (3 m). It interfaces with your existing tanks and pumps where possible. Mounting hardware adapts to common toolbar and 3-point hitch configurations — we validate fit during early access onboarding.

What are the key performance specs?

3 m bar width, 12–16 nozzles at 25 cm pitch, design ground speed of 6 km/h, target coverage of 1.8 ha/hr, and valve actuation latency of 6–12 ms. Full architecture details are on the RK-SPRAY product page.

Does it require internet or cell service?

No. Perception, valve control, and application logging run entirely on the edge. Mission data syncs when connectivity is available — the tractor keeps spraying when it is not.

What is the safety architecture?

A dedicated safety MCU runs outside the main Linux compute stack. It monitors pressure, enclosure integrity, and software heartbeat. Any fault forces valves, pumps, and lasers to a safe state independently of the perception pipeline.

What regulatory requirements apply?

Ground spray applications are subject to EPA requirements and state pesticide applicator regulations. Operators are responsible for licensing, record-keeping, and label compliance. Rockio systems log valve events and coverage data to support your records.

Which farm management systems do you integrate with?

REST API and standard export formats. Direct integrations for John Deere Operations Center and custom ERP connections are available on request during early access.

How is field data handled?

Perception and application data are processed under contract with the operator. We apply data minimization, encryption at rest and in transit, and role-based access controls. See our Privacy policy for details.

Is RK-SPRAY available for purchase today?

We are in active development and structured field trials with select partners. Request early access on the product page or contact us directly.
