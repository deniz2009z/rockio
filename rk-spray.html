01

The problem with normal sprayers

A typical sprayer treats the whole row the same — weeds, crop, and bare dirt all get chemical. You pay for the full tank even when only a few spots needed it.

RK-SPRAY does the opposite. It looks at each plant, skips the healthy ones, and sprays only the weeds. You use what you need. Nothing more.

02

How it works — step by step

Watch the animation above, or read it here:

  • Step 1 — Drives the row

    The machine rolls through your field on its own, following the crop line.

  • Step 2 — Looks at each plant

    Cameras on the front see what's crop and what's a weed. It works even when it's dusty.

  • Step 3 — Sprays only the weeds

    A small nozzle turns on for a split second when it sees a weed. Your crop is never touched.

  • Step 4 — Saves a record

    Every spray is logged — where, when, how much. Ready when you need your spray paperwork.

04

The machine — plain facts

  • How wide?

    About 10 feet per pass. Fits standard rows in corn, soy, grapes, and orchards.

  • How much does it hold?

    120 gallons. Enough for up to 8 hours depending on how many weeds you have.

  • How does it spray?

    12 small nozzles under the machine. Each one turns on only when it sees a weed.

  • Does it need internet?

    No. It makes all decisions on the machine. Works with no cell signal in the field.

  • How do I see what it did?

    On your phone or laptop when you're back at the house. Every spray is listed.

  • Do you help me set it up?

    Yes. We come to your farm, run the first passes with you, and train your crew.
