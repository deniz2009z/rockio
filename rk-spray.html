01

Intelligence bar retrofit

RK-SPRAY is designed as an intelligence bar that mounts directly to a tractor's existing toolbar or 3-point hitch. Use your existing tanks and pumps where possible — or run on the integrated fluid stack below.

02

System architecture

Four subsystems. One closed loop from perception to fluid delivery.

  • Compute node

    NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano Super as the central anchor for real-time edge perception and deterministic valve control. 7–25 W power envelope. Up to 67 TOPS AI throughput.

  • Perception stack

    Global-shutter stereo cameras (Intel RealSense D455 class or custom Luxonis global-shutter configurations). Global shutter is required to eliminate motion artifacts from tractor vibration and high-speed ground travel — rolling shutter is not sufficient.

  • Actuation & fluidics

    Closed-loop pressure system with industrial fast-acting PWM solenoid valves (TeeJet Dynajet-class or equivalent individual nozzle control) integrated into a custom manifold.

  • Safety layer

    Dedicated MCU or safety PLC running independently of Linux user-space. Hardware watchdog forces safe state — all valves, pumps, and lasers off — on any pressure anomaly, enclosure breach, or software timeout.

04

Technical specifications

  • Physical dimensions

    3-meter horizontal bar. 12 to 16 independently controlled micro-spray nozzles spaced at 25 cm pitch.

  • Throughput

    Ground cruise speed of 6 km/h (1.67 m/s). At 3 m bar width, effective coverage rate of 1.8 hectares per hour.

  • Valve latency

    6–12 ms response on a 20 Hz PWM control system. At 6 km/h, 12 ms latency limits spatial execution error to 2.0 cm — matching centimeter-scale weed targets.

  • Drift mitigation

    Nozzles at 30–35 cm above target zone. Fluid calibrated for coarse droplets in the 200–300 µm range. Opaque mechanical shroud acts as a wind lockout — validated in crosswinds up to 3 m/s.

  • Power budget

    150–200 W total during full operation: Jetson Orin Nano 7–25 W · sensors & cameras 3–10 W · fluid pump 60–120 W (duty-cycle dependent) · valves, relays, and active LED lighting from remainder.

  • Integration

    Mounts to standard tractor toolbar or 3-point hitch. Compatible with existing tanks and pumps, or integrated fluid stack.
