Operating principle

Most ag-tech optimizes for demos. We optimize for field conditions — dust, heat, connectivity gaps, and regulatory scrutiny. Every system we ship is built to hold under load.

01

The field does not forgive approximation.

Conventional spraying treats every hectare the same. Chemical goes everywhere. Costs scale linearly. Records are manual. Operators inherit the risk.

Rockio builds machines that treat every plant as a discrete decision — sensing, classifying, and applying in a single autonomous pass.

02

What we build

End-to-end autonomous systems for precision agriculture — from onboard perception to compliance export. One stack. Field to console.

  • 01

    Perceive

    Multi-modal sensing fuses LiDAR, stereo vision, and odometry into a real-time field model. Works without GPS.

  • 02

    Classify

    Edge inference identifies crop, weed, pest, and bare soil per plant. Decisions at 30 Hz, not zone averages.

  • 03

    Actuate

    Individually controlled nozzles deliver variable-rate application within milliseconds of target detection.

  • 04

    Verify

    Every application logged with coordinates, volume, and chemical type. Audit-ready for EPA and state reporting.

Technology stack

Vertical integration from sensor to console. No black boxes. No vendor lock-in on your field data.

Perception

Multi-modal sensing

Dual 128-beam LiDAR, stereo RGB + NIR, IMU fusion. Operates in dust, low light, and GPS-denied environments.

Compute

Edge inference

Onboard GPU at 30 TOPS. Custom crop classifiers with sub-12 ms actuation loop. No cloud dependency for operation.

Actuation

Precision delivery

12 solenoid nozzles with variable-rate control. 150–300 μm droplet size. Per-nozzle flow metering.
