01

Who we are

Rockio is an agricultural robotics company based in San Francisco. We design and build perception, compute, and spray-control systems for commercial growers — not as a replacement for your operation, but as a layer of intelligence on top of it.

Our focus: turn real-time computer vision into millisecond valve control under real field conditions — vibration, dust, speed, and pressure swings included.

02

Why we exist

Blanket spraying treats every inch of a field the same. Most of the chemical never needed to be there. Growers pay for full-tank passes, carry compliance burden, and accept drift and crop stress as the cost of doing business.

We believe the fix is not a bigger sprayer — it is better decisions, faster, at the nozzle.

03

What we are building

Our first product is an intelligence bar — a retrofit module that mounts to a tractor toolbar or 3-point hitch. It uses your existing tanks and pumps where possible, and adds the sensing, compute, and individual nozzle control needed for spot application.

  • See at speed

    Global-shutter stereo vision that holds up under tractor vibration and ground travel — not rolling-shutter cameras that smear in the field.

  • Decide on the edge

    Onboard compute runs perception and valve logic locally. No cloud round-trip. No cell signal required.

  • Act in milliseconds

    Individual PWM solenoid valves fire in 6–12 ms — tight enough to hit centimeter-scale targets at field speed.

  • Fail safe

    A dedicated safety controller runs outside Linux. Any pressure fault, enclosure breach, or software timeout forces valves, pumps, and lasers to a safe state.

05

How we work

  • Engineering-first

    We spec from physics constraints — valve latency, droplet size, release height, power budget — then build hardware that meets them in the field.

  • Field partnerships

    We develop alongside growers and operators. Systems are tuned on real rows, real crops, and real spray days — not only in the lab.

  • Retrofit, not rip-and-replace

    The intelligence bar integrates with existing tractors, toolbars, and fluid systems. Your capital equipment stays in service.
