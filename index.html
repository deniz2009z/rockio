How it works

Scan. Assign. Run.

No manual routing. No guessing which block needs work.

  1. 01 Scan the field

    A mapping unit drives or flies the perimeter and builds a full acre-by-acre map — boundaries, rows, obstacles.

  2. 02 Saved to the app

    The map lands in Rockio Fleet on your phone. Blocks, coordinates, and status stay updated automatically.

  3. 03 Robots do the work

    You assign a job — spray Block A, plow Block C. The right machine goes out and executes without a driver in the cab.

  4. 04 They coordinate

    Units share the same map. A sprayer knows where the scout already passed. No overlap, no missed rows.
