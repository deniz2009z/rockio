01

Most sprayers waste your money.

When you spray a whole field, most of the chemical lands where nothing is wrong — on healthy plants and empty ground. Only a few spots actually had weeds. You still paid for the full tank.

Think of it this way: if only 3 plants in a row have weeds, a normal sprayer treats all 10. You pay for 10. Rockio treats 3. You pay for 3.

02

What Rockio does — in 4 steps

You don't need to be a tech person or a farmer to get this. Here's the whole idea:

  • 1. Drives down your row

    The machine rolls through your field on its own — like a slow mower following the crop line.

  • 2. Looks at what's in front of it

    It has cameras that see each plant. It can tell the difference between your crop and a weed — even when it's dusty out.

  • 3. Sprays only the weeds

    When it sees a weed, a small nozzle turns on for a split second. Your healthy plants never get hit.

  • 4. Writes down what it did

    Every spray is saved — where, when, how much. So when someone asks for your spray records, you already have them.
