Privacy policy

How Rockio Inc. collects, uses, and protects personal information.

1. Who we are

Rockio Inc., 548 Market Street, Suite 35410, San Francisco, CA 94104, USA. Contact: privacy@rockio.com

2. Information we collect

When you contact us, subscribe to our newsletter, or use our forms, we may collect your name, email address, organization, phone number, and message content. We collect this information to respond to inquiries and provide our services.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate this website. Optional analytics and marketing cookies may be used with your consent. Manage preferences via in the footer.

4. Field and operation data

Rockio systems may collect during operation:

  • Ground imagery and video from onboard cameras (RGB, depth)
  • GPS position data and application logs
  • Sensor readings (pressure, flow, valve events, equipment status)
  • Operational metadata (timestamps, field boundaries, operator IDs)

This data is processed primarily to deliver our services to customers. Processing occurs under customer agreements with data minimization, retention limits, and encryption applied.

Customers are responsible for compliance with applicable federal and state regulations, including EPA requirements for pesticide application and state applicator licensing.

5. How we use information

We use personal information to provide and improve our products, communicate with you, comply with legal obligations, and protect our rights. We do not sell personal information.

6. California residents (CCPA)

California residents may request access to, deletion of, or information about personal information we collect. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@rockio.com. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

7. Data security

We use TLS encryption, access controls, and role-based permissions. Field data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

8. Retention

Contact and inquiry data is retained as long as necessary to fulfill the request and meet legal requirements, typically up to three years.

9. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be posted on this page.
