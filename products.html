02

Platform architecture

Every Rockio system shares a common stack — perceive at speed, decide on the edge, actuate in milliseconds, and fail safe.

01 · Perceive

Global-shutter stereo

Machine vision that holds up under tractor vibration and ground travel. Not rolling-shutter smear at field speed.

02 · Compute

Edge inference

Jetson-class onboard compute runs perception and control locally. No cloud round-trip. No cell signal required.

03 · Actuate

Millisecond valves

Individual PWM solenoid nozzles fire in 6–12 ms — centimeter-scale targeting at 6 km/h ground speed.

04 · Safeguard

Independent safety MCU

Dedicated controller outside Linux. Pressure faults, enclosure breach, or heartbeat loss force a safe state.
